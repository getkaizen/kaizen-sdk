# Code of Conduct

We follow the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/). In short:

- Be respectful and inclusive.
- Provide and accept constructive feedback.
- Gracefully accept responsibility and apologize when necessary.
- Focus on what is best for the community and the project.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to **conduct@kaizen**. All complaints will be reviewed promptly and fairly.
